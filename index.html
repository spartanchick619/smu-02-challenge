About Me

Hello! I am Rosa Valdez, CMT, HHP. Growing up, I knew I wanted to help people. My passion to help people was what guided me to pursue massage therapy. I have been a massage therapist for over 9 years. My second passion is learning and that lead me to pursue a degree and learning somato-emotional coaching. Having worked in small offices to then a world renowned health facility, I was able to work with over 2,000 clients. Being an entrepreneur, I had my own practice and focused on energy work and BodyMind Coaching. Unfortunately, due to Covid-19 in 2020, I had to close my business indefinitely.

The world changed and I knew I had to adapt with it. After much research, I decided to break into one of the fastest growing industries. The goals I have for the future are to learn full stack software development and then utilize my skills and experience to align with a company to assist in offering great products to companies and the public. A company that I aspire to work with is Insomniac Events. They have created the biggest festivals in EDM. Down the line, I would like to pour back into the community and possibly teach and mentor women and people from marginalized communities and help them start in the tech industry.
